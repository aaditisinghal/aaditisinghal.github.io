Resilience

A Developer's Journey | 2024

Through sleepless nights, the code would flow,

A dream of healing, bright and grand.

Yet words I spoke, too fast, too low,

Were lost like footprints in the sand.

A stranger's tongue, though mine to claim,

Became a wall I could not breach.

Each echoed "pardon" fed the flame,

Of lessons failure came to teach.

The judges turned, their nods grew cold,

A prize now slipping from my hand.

Yet in my heart, I still held gold,

The will to rise, the strength to stand.

A poem about perseverance and growth

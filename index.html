About Me

I'm a Presidential Scholar and Honors student at Georgia State University, pursuing a Bachelor's in Computer Science with a minor in Biology. My passion lies at the intersection of artificial intelligence and biotechnology, where I conduct cutting-edge research in transcriptomics and develop innovative healthcare solutions.

As a published researcher and hackathon winner, I've built AI-driven platforms that predict diseases from metabolite analysis, created AR nutrition assistants, and developed neural networks for biological image analysis.
